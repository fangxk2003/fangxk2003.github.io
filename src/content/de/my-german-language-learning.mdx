This page is the beginning of my German language learning notes.

I want to use this space to collect vocabulary, grammar patterns, listening practice, mistakes I notice often, and short reflections from the learning process.

## Why German?

German feels precise, structured, and sometimes delightfully complicated. Learning it is also a way to understand more of European academic life, culture, and everyday communication.

## What I Want To Track

1. Useful words and phrases from real situations.
2. Grammar patterns that finally start to make sense.
3. Reading and listening resources that are worth revisiting.
4. Small writing exercises in German.

## First Goal

My first goal is simple: build a steady habit and make the language feel less distant.
