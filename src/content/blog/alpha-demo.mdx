## 1. The Mathematical Foundation

To train the model effectively, we had to minimize the divergence between the policy and the target distribution. The core loss function is defined as:

$$
\mathcal{L}(\theta) = \mathbb{E}_{t} \left[ \nabla_\theta \log \pi_\theta(a_t | s_t) \hat{A}_t \right]
$$

This ensures that the agent learns from statistically significant deviations.
